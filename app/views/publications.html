April-ANN

April-ann toolkit (A Pattern Recognizer In Lua with Artificial Neural Networks). Is a toolkit that incorporates ANN algorithms (as dropout, stacked denoising auto-encoders, convolutional neural networks), with other pattern recognition methods as hiddem makov models (HMMs). I have been using and contributing this amazing tool during my research.
